About Us

Welcome to Harmony Events !


Harmony Events was born out of a passion for creating extraordinary moments. Our founder, Barat Kumar Mondi, saw a gap in the market for personalized event planning while organizing her own destination wedding. Whether it’s a wedding that makes you weep with joy or a corporate event that dazzles your clients, we sprinkle a little magic on everything we touch.

Our Mission :

To create unforgettable experiences that leave you and your guests talking for years. We believe every event should be as unique as the people it celebrates.

Our Values :

We value creativity, passion, and a touch of chaos (the good kind!). Our team is dedicated to turning your wildest dreams into reality, even if that means wrestling with a giant inflatable unicorn. Let’s make some unforgettable memories together!
